This program shows object oriented programming in C++ by creation of complex class. Complex numbers are represented through two private data members which are real part and imaginary part. In program, default constructor, parameterized constructor, setter functions, accessor functions, calculation functions, display functions, and overloaded mathematical operators are included. With help of class, complex number’s magnitude, its angle in degrees is calculated, and complex conjugate is returned. Complex number is also displayed in rectangular form and in polar form by it. By overloaded operators, complex objects can be added, subtracted, multiplied, and divided through mathematical symbols.
